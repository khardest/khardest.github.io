Comparing Boy Band’s Successes to their Members Solo Careers

By Keira Hardesty

The music industry has seen it time and time again. Boy bands break up and their members go on to start solo careers.

Two famous boy bands, *NSYNC and One Direction had successful careers before they broke up. From both bands emerged solo stars, such as Harry Styles and Justin Timberlake.

*NSYNC formed in 1995 and was made up of five members: Justin Timberlake, JC Chasez, Lance Bass, Joey Fatone and Chris Kirkpatrick. They released six albums and completed five nationwide concert tours.

The band has sold over 70 million records, making them one of the best-selling boy bands of all time. Two of the former members, Timberlake and Chasez, started solo careers.

One Direction made music for six years beginning in 2010. The band’s members were Harry Styles, Niall Horan, Louis Tomlinson, Liam Payne and Zayn Malik. Malik left the band prior to the breakup in 2015 for personal reasons.

They released five albums and went on four world tours during their career. After the breakup all members started solo careers. This article will focus on the careers of Harry Styles and Liam Payne.

*NSYNC, Justin Timberlake and JC Chasez

In the first two years of their career, *NSYNC appeared on the Billboard Hot 100 Top 50 for approximately 70 weeks. Their first song in the Top 50 came on March 7, 1998, when "I Want You Back" reached number 25. Over this period, four different songs charted, with their highest peak being number 2 for "Music of My Heart."

Justin Timberlake launched his solo career in 2002. In the following two years, he appeared in the Billboard Top 50 for 68 individual weeks. His first solo entry was "Like I Love You," which debuted at number 46. During this time, four of his songs charted, with "Cry Me a River" achieving his highest position at number 3.

JC Chasez also started a solo career, appearing in the Top 50 seven times within his first two years. His one song that charted, "Blowin' Me Up (With Her Love)," first entered at number 45 and eventually peaked at number 35.

One Direction, Harry Styles and Liam Payne

From 2012 to 2014, One Direction appeared in the top 50 88 times. Ten of their songs appeared on the chart, with their debut single “What Makes You Beautiful” first charting at 28, and “Best Song Ever” reaching the highest position at number 2.

In his first two years as a solo artist, Harry Styles entered the Billboard Top 50 twelve times, led by his debut single “Sign of the Times,” which peaked at number 4.

Liam Payne also made a strong start. His debut single, "Strip That Down," debuted at number 42, peaked at number 12, and spent 27 weeks on the chart.

Top 10 Hits

When considering the success of the bands and solo careers, it is also important to consider the amount of songs that reached the top 10 on the Billboard Hot 100 over their time as artists.

*NSYNC achieved six top 10 hits during their nine-year career, averaging one every 1.5 years. Justin Timberlake has had 19 songs in the top 10 over his 23-year solo career, roughly one every year and a few months. JC Chasez has never had a song in the top 10.

During One Direction's five year career, they had six top 10 hits, meaning they averaged over one top ten hit for each year of their career. Harry Styles shares a similar statistic as during his career so far, which has been seven years, he has had seven songs reach the top 10. Liam Payne only had one top 10 hit during his six year career.

Conclusion

While not the only factor, this data can indicate that the original success of the solo members can be partially attributed to the fame they gained during their time as boy band members. However, the two also put in work themselves to grow into the stars they are today.
